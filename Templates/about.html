About Citizen AI

Citizen AI is an intelligent citizen engagement platform designed to revolutionize how governments interact with the public — combining conversational AI, sentiment analysis, and an analytics dashboard to turn feedback into action.

Built with Flask, IBM Granite models, and IBM Watson services, Citizen AI automates routine interactions, surfaces emerging issues, and helps public agencies respond faster and more transparently.

How it works

Citizen AI receives citizen queries (from web forms, chat widgets, or social channels), extracts intent and entities using IBM Granite models, enriches results with IBM Watson services (like Discovery or Natural Language Understanding), and responds with templated or generated replies. Feedback is routed to the analytics dashboard and case management workflows as needed.

  • 1. Intake: Multi-channel collection (web, mobile, social).
  • 2. Understanding: Intent & entity extraction via Granite models.
  • 3. Analysis: Sentiment scoring and trend detection.
  • 4. Response & Escalation: Auto-replies, case creation, or handoff to human agents.
Tech stack

Backend: Flask (Python)

AI: IBM Granite models, IBM Watson NLU/Discovery

Frontend: Bootstrap + vanilla JS (or React/Gradio for demos)

Data: PostgreSQL / Elasticsearch / Timeseries DB (optional)

Use cases

Citizen support automation

Automate common queries about documentation, payments, and appointment scheduling to reduce response times and staff load.

Policy feedback & public consultation

Collect and analyze public sentiment during consultations to inform policy drafting and stakeholder outreach.
